One-Arm Palm-Down Wrist Curl

10 Reps x 4 Sets

The One-Arm Palm-Down Wrist Curl is a strength training exercise that targets the muscles in the forearm. It involves holding a weight with your palm facing down and curling your wrist upward, working the muscles responsible for wrist flexion.

10 Reps x 4 Sets

The Palms-Down Wrist Curl is a forearm exercise where you lift a weight with your palms facing down, emphasizing wrist flexion. This exercise targets the muscles on the underside of your forearm and helps improve grip strength.

Palms-Up Wrist Curl

10 Reps x 4 Sets

The Palms-Up Wrist Curl is a forearm exercise in which you lift a weight with your palms facing up. This movement primarily targets the muscles on the top side of your forearm, emphasizing wrist extension. It's commonly performed to strengthen the forearm muscles and improve overall wrist flexibility and strength.
