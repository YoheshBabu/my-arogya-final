Front Raise

10 Reps x 4 Sets

The Front Raise is a weightlifting exercise that primarily targets the front (anterior) part of the shoulder muscles. In this exercise, you lift a weight (dumbbell or barbell) directly in front of you, keeping your arms straight. It's commonly used to strengthen and tone the anterior deltoids.

Lateral Raise

10 Reps x 4 Sets

The Lateral Raise is a weightlifting exercise that targets the lateral (side) part of the shoulder muscles. In this exercise, you typically lift weights (dumbbells) to the side, away from your body, keeping your arms straight or slightly bent. Lateral Raises are effective for isolating and strengthening the lateral deltoids, contributing to overall shoulder development.

10 Reps x 4 Sets

The Palms-In Alternated Shoulder Press is an exercise where you lift weights (dumbbells or other resistance) overhead while alternating between palms-in (facing each other) and palms-forward positions. This movement targets the shoulder muscles and can help enhance overall shoulder strength and stability.
